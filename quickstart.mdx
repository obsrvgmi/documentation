## Getting Started

> **Note:** Decibel is currently on **Aptos testnet**. 

### `/mint`
To begin trading, you will need testnet funds. Use this command to mint testnet USDC directly to your trading wallet. 

---

## Trading & Management

### `/markets`
View the pulse of the market instantly. Get a snapshot of all listed pairs on Aptos, including real-time prices, 24h percentage changes, and current funding rates to spot opportunities.

### `/limit`
Execute with precision. Set limit orders to open long or short positions at specific price points. This allows you to catch wicks and enter trades exactly where you planned without staring at the screen.

### `/positions`
Your mission control. Monitor all your active trades in one view, including live PnL, entry price, liquidation price, and current leverage. Use this to track performance and manage risk at a glance.

### `/close`
Secure profits or cut losses instantly. This command brings up a menu to close specific positions or all active trades at market price. You can also select to close partial amounts (e.g., 50%) of a position.

### `/orders`
Manage your order book. View a list of all your pending limit and trigger orders. Use this to cancel outdated setups or modify open orders to align with your current market outlook.

### `/tp`
Lock in gains automatically. Set a **Take Profit** trigger for an open position. When the market hits your target price, the bot will automatically close the trade to secure your profit.

### `/sl`
Protect your capital. Set a **Stop Loss** trigger to automatically close your position if the market moves against you. This is an essential tool for disciplined risk management and capital preservation.

### `/chart`
Visualize price action without leaving the chat. Generates an instant candlestick chart for any requested pair, complete with customizable timeframes and key indicators to analyze trends on the fly.

### `/settings`
Customize your trading engine. Adjust your default leverage, slippage tolerance, gas presets, and notification preferences to tailor the Decibel bot to your specific trading style.

## Social & PVP

### `/track`
Spy on the whales and top traders. Enter a wallet address to receive real-time notifications whenever that trader opens or closes a position, allowing you to copy-trade or counter-trade their moves.

### `/untrack`
Refine your signal feed. Stop following specific wallets or traders that you previously tracked. Use this to clear the noise and focus only on the most profitable wallets in your feed.

### `/alert`
Never miss a move. Set custom price alerts for any asset. The bot will send you a direct message immediately when your target price is hit, so you can react to volatility instantly.

### `/watchlist`
Your personalized market view. Curate a favorites list of tokens to monitor closely. This gives you quick access to prices and stats for your preferred assets without searching the full market list.

### `/taunt`
Flex on the competition. Send a playful, broadcasted message to the feed or a specific user after a successful trade or liquidation. This adds a social, competitive PVP layer to the trading experience.

### `/guild`
Strength in numbers. Create, join, or manage a trading guild. Pool your trading volume with friends to compete in team leaderboards, unlock collective rewards, and dominate the Decibel arena.
