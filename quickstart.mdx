## Getting Started

> **Note:** Decibel is currently on **Aptos testnet**.

### `/mint`
Use this command to get free test funds to start trading. Since we are on the Aptos testnet, you will receive **$10,000 in testnet USDC** to practice with risk-free.

## Trading & Management

### `/long`
Open a long position immediately at the current market price. Use this if you believe the token's price will **go up**.

### `/short`
Open a short position immediately at the current market price. Use this if you believe the token's price will **go down**.

### `/markets`
Check the live status of the market. This shows you a list of all available tokens, their current prices, and how much they have changed in the last 24 hours.

### `/limit`
Set an order to buy or sell at a specific price. Your trade will not open until the market hits the exact price you chose.

### `/positions`
View all your active trades in one place. You can see your profit or loss, your entry price, and how close you are to liquidation.

### `/close`
Close your active trades immediately to take profit or stop a loss. You can choose to close a specific trade or all of them at once.

### `/orders`
View a list of your waiting orders (like limit orders). Use this to see what is pending or to cancel an order you don't want anymore.

### `/tp`
Set a "Take Profit" price. The bot will automatically close your trade when the price hits this target to secure your winnings.

### `/sl`
Set a "Stop Loss" price. The bot will automatically close your trade if the price goes against you to prevent you from losing too much money.

### `/chart`
Get a price chart sent directly to the chat. You can select different tokens and timeframes to see how the price is moving.

### `/settings`
Change your personal preferences. Use this to adjust your default leverage, notifications, and other trading options to fit your style.

## Social & PVP

### `/track`
Follow another trader's wallet address. The bot will send you a message whenever that person opens or closes a trade so you can see their moves.

### `/untrack`
Stop following a wallet. Use this command if you no longer want to receive notifications about a specific trader's activity.

### `/alert`
Set a custom price alarm. The bot will verify the price and send you a direct message as soon as a token hits the number you selected.

### `/watchlist`
Create a list of your favorite tokens. This helps you quickly check prices for the coins you trade most often without searching through the full list.

### `/taunt`
Send a fun public message to celebrate a big win or tease other traders. This adds a competitive game-like feel to the group.

### `/guild`
Create or join a team of traders. You can group up with friends to compete for higher rankings and win team rewards.
